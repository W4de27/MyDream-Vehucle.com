Toyota Supra

General Information

Model: Toyota Supra (World Record Edition)

Type: High-Performance Custom Sports Car

Engine: 3.0L Inline-6 Turbocharged (Heavily Modified)

Power: 1,200+ hp

Top Speed: 240+ mph

0-60 mph: 1.9 seconds

Full Informations

The Toyota Supra is a popular platform for tuning, and some enthusiasts and professionals have taken this iconic car to extreme levels of performance, setting world records in the process. Among the most famous examples are custom-built Supras that have broken speed and acceleration records, often in drag racing or top speed events.

One of the most notable custom Supras is a heavily modified version that produced over 1,200 horsepower. This level of power was achieved through a comprehensive overhaul of the engine, including a massive single turbo conversion, reinforced internals, upgraded fuel systems, and advanced tuning. The result was a Supra capable of reaching speeds well over 240 mph, with some builds even pushing toward the 250 mph mark.

This world record Supra was also known for its blistering acceleration, with a 0-60 mph time of just 1.9 seconds, putting it in the same league as some of the fastest hypercars in the world. Such performance is typically achieved in a controlled environment, often on a drag strip, where the Supra's immense power can be fully unleashed.

To handle this power, the car was equipped with a specialized transmission, often a race-spec sequential gearbox, and a custom-built differential. The chassis was reinforced, and advanced aerodynamics were added to maintain stability at high speeds. These modifications ensure that the car can safely and effectively handle the extreme forces generated at these performance levels.

The interior of this record-breaking Supra was stripped down to essentials, with weight reduction in mind. It featured racing seats, a roll cage, and a digital display for monitoring engine parameters. Every aspect of the car was optimized for performance, sacrificing comfort for the sake of speed and power.

This Toyota Supra stands as a testament to the incredible potential of tuning and engineering, showcasing what can be achieved when pushing the boundaries of automotive performance. It is a legend in the world of high-performance tuning, inspiring countless enthusiasts and builders around the globe.

Full Informations

The Mercedes-AMG GT is a stunning high-performance sports car known for its luxurious design and impressive performance. In its custom, high-performance edition, the AMG GT reaches new heights of power and speed, showcasing AMG's dedication to engineering excellence and driving thrills.

This modified Mercedes-AMG GT features a 4.0-liter V8 engine, twin-turbocharged and extensively upgraded to produce over 850 horsepower. The modifications include larger turbochargers, a high-performance intercooler, a bespoke exhaust system, and a recalibrated ECU. These enhancements allow the AMG GT to achieve top speeds exceeding 210 mph, placing it among the elite of high-performance sports cars.

The acceleration of this high-performance AMG GT is equally remarkable, with a 0-60 mph time of just 2.9 seconds. This rapid acceleration is achieved through a refined 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, optimized gear ratios, and AMG's advanced traction control systems. The result is a car that delivers explosive acceleration and precise handling, whether on the track or the road.

To manage the increased power and speed, the AMG GT is equipped with advanced aerodynamic features. These include a custom front splitter, rear diffuser, and an active rear wing, all designed to enhance downforce and stability at high speeds. The suspension system is also upgraded with race-tuned components, including adjustable dampers and a lowered ride height, providing sharp handling and exceptional ride quality.

Inside, the Mercedes-AMG GT offers a driver-focused interior that combines luxury with performance. The cabin features high-quality materials such as Nappa leather, carbon fiber accents, and Alcantara trim. The cockpit includes lightweight sports seats, a flat-bottom steering wheel, and a digital display that provides real-time performance data. The interior is designed to provide a balance of comfort and connectivity, ensuring that drivers are fully engaged with the car.

This Mercedes-AMG GT high-performance edition exemplifies AMG's commitment to creating vehicles that offer both luxury and exhilarating performance. It stands as a testament to advanced engineering and design, delivering a driving experience that is both thrilling and refined.
